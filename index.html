Nominate School

India’s largest parent-school study, covering over 20,000 parents and 1000+ schools across Karnataka.

Aims to recognize extraordinary commitment by schools towards 360-degree student growth and development.

Distinguished and eminent personalities across different sectors comprise the National & Regional Advisory Councils for the Awards.

The outcome will provide the state and community with valuable insights into the current practices and evolving Innovations in the Education Sector.

The Best Practices will be published by the Consortium for Data Science in Education.

To create a responsible, well-informed and empowered generation for the future, the growth and development of a child must be a collaboration of parents, teachers and institutions. With this objective, seven categories have been chosen for the Awards.

Innovation in Student Health & Safety

Innovation in Parental Engagement

Innovation in Information & Communication Technology

Innovation in Co-curricular Development

Innovation in Teaching & Learning Methods

Innovation in Inculcating Value Education

Nominate School

NOMINATION

Principals or representatives of the participating schools  across Karnataka must fill the nomination form. Please click the button below to submit your nomination.

Submit nomination

EVALUATION

The nominations will have a filtering process based on the selection criteria. The respective school representatives will be interviewed and the innovative practices will be documented and presented to the Jury. The Jury will select the winners under each category based on their evaluation.

PUBLICATION

Based on the inputs from the school, as well as the inputs from parents of each school, the Best Practices implemented by various schools will be published by the Consortium for Data Science in Education.

AWARDS

All participating schools are invited to the Awards Event, the dates of which will be announced shortly. Two awards will be conferred under each category at this event. The winning schools as decided by Jury decision and parent votes will be felicitated.

Pupilsight is a Data Analytics platform designed keeping in mind the unique needs and challenges faced by Educators. In the age of drones and space travel, kids and youth now have access to better learning than ever before. In comparison, the ways in which educators make decisions remains painfully unevolved. Pupilsight’s mission is to provide these decisive insights to institutions which seek better decision making as they prepare children for a rapidly changing society.
